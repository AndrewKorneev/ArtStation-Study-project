Enviroment Concept art & Design

Environment concept art is a type of visual art and design. Concept artists use their skills to visualize ideas for the environment than the production team needs in the game or movie they are working on. The environment can be anything from an abandoned space station, an island in the ocean, a medieval castle, etc.
